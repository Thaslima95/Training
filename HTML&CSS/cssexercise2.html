CROISSANT

A croissant is a buttery inspired by the shape of the Austrian kipferl but using the French yeast-leavened laminated dough.

BROWNIE

A chocolate brownie, or simply a brownie, is a chocolate baked confection. Brownies come in a variety of forms.

DOUGHNUTS

A doughnut or donut is a type of food made from leavened fried dough.
